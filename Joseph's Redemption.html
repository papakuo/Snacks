    43. Joseph's Redemption (Genesus 42: 18 - 25)

    The Starter

    It's hard to forgive. It's even harder to forget. This is true for both the offensive and offended parties. Even though Joseph suffered a lot because of what his brothers did to him, his brothers also suffered because they couldn't forget what they had done to Joseph. So, how did Joseph redeem himself when he finally got the opportunity? As saved believers, how do we redeem ourselves from this kind of situation?
