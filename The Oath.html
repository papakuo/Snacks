    12. The Oath (Hebrews 6: 13 - 20)

    The Starter

    God promised Abraham to become a big nation with countless descendants and vast lands not only with covenants but also guaranteed it with an oath. Indeed, that's what had happened. After being saved, we are now the descendants of Abraham by faith. So, does the oath still apply to us? If so, then do we know what God wants us to become, and how?
