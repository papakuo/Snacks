    23. Abel and Enoch - Faith (Hebrews 11: 1 - 6)

    The Starter

    Seeing is believing. That's how we built our beliefs. For the unseen, we derive our beliefs from what we have seen. For the invisible God, we have seen His creations. Why are there still so many unbelievers, even atheists? Israelites had seen God’s wonders and miracles throughout their history, yet God said that they still didn’t believe Him. Why?
