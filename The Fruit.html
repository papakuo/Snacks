    33. The Fruit (Hebrews 12: 11 - 17)

    The Starter

    Adam and Eve ate the fruit from the tree of knowledge of good and evil. So, our sinful flesh could only yield the deceitful evil fruit. God's disciplines would train us to free from our old flesh to yield the peaceful fruit of righteousness. It's a painful total life transformation inside out. So, how do we accommodate it in life and know what fruit to yield?
