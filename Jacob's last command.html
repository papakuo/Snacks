    52. Jacob's last command (Genesis 49: 29 - 33)

    The Starter

    Even though Jacob was energetic, aggressive and spiritual all his life, he still needed to face the final end like any mortals. Yet he faced his final days with a content and peaceful mind. He acted as if he had finished a journey and could rest in peace now. This was like the final days of some of Christ's disciples. The same rest is prepared for us who are saved too. So, how do we claim the assurance now?
