    37. The Camp (Hebrews 13: 8 - 13)

    The Starter

    Each of us has a comfort zone for our flesh. Even though the comfort zone could be different, it could be developed by us to a strong camp, or even a fortress. So, oftentimes, it becomes an obstacle for us following Christ. So, do we know about our own camp and how to overcome it?
