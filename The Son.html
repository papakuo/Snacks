    1. The Son (Hebrews 1: 1 - 4)

    The Starter

    We used to trust our own instinct based on seeing and believing ideas. Then we learned that neither is reliable because they too could be varied. So, to find the truth we need to find a source unchangeable. It's obvious not in our own instinct nor in this world. If we search for the truth in the Bible, then the Book of Hebrews is a good start. It teaches us about faith and where it comes from. The first thing to know is the Son of God. Why is that?
