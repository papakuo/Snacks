    45. Judah's Devotion (Genesis 44: 27 - 34)

    The Starter

    Judah didn't seem to be ambitious or outstanding among his brothers. Even his two older sons were struck by God to death because of their evil deeds. Yet, later, Jacob prophesied that Christ would be Judah's descendent. Indeed, that's how it was fulfilled. So, what made Judah so special? How could we learn from him?
