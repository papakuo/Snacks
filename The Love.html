    36. The Love (Hebrews 13: 1 - 7)

    The Starter

    God is love. So, the Bible teaches us to love one another. However, since we are still in this flesh, loving one another is not as easy as we thought, a clear cut. The act of love could cause unexpected consequences or even chaos. So, should we still show our love without much consideration? Or should we use our own judgement and be more conservative?
