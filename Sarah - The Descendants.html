    25. Sarah - The Descendants (Hebrews 11: 11 - 12)

    The Starter

    Faith is available to people who are willing to submit themselves to God. When we talked about faith, we often thought of Abraham. The pressure was on Sarah too because she needed to endure the same trials as Abraham's wife. To give Abraham descendants, Sarah let Abraham receive her maid Hagar because she was too old to bear a child herself. So, was Sarah lacking faith? Or, because of her submission that God promised her to conceive?
