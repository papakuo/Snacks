    31. The Founder and Perfecter (Hebrews 12: 1 - 2)

    The Starter

    In Jericho, Jesus said to Zacchaeus that for the Son of Man came to seek and to save the lost when Zacchaeus promised to give to the poor and restore fourfold to whom he had defrauded after seeing Jesus. So, if we lay aside every weight and sin like Zacchaeus then would Christ come to seek and save us? How do we look to Jesus, the founder and perfecter of our faith for that?
