    5. Noah's Faith (Genesis 7: 11 - 13)

    The Starter

    We often read the news about the missing Noah's ark. Or people even tried to duplicate Noah's ark. No doubt that people were so amazed by the profound works of Noah's ark. However, the real question is what was the driving force for a person to accomplish such a task? What could we learn from it? Could we apply it to ourselves?
