    8. Another Day (Hebrews 4: 6 - 10)

    The Starter

    Joshua promised some Israelis to help their brothers to enter Canaan, then God would give rest to both their brothers and them. Yet, according to the Bible, Joshua's rest didn't last long because of Israelis' disobedience. In the Bible, there are typologies and substances. Could it be that Joshua's rest was a typology? If so, then what's the substance of the true rest?
