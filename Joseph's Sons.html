    41. Joseph's Sons (Genesis 41: 50 - 53)

    The Starter

    Joseph had two sons. He named his first son to forget the hardship, and his second son for the fruitfulness. Both names were to remember what God had done for him. When Jesus told us to carry our own cross to follow him, we know that he wanted us to endure the same hardship as him. So, how do we ensure that we could endure the hardship then prevail like Joseph afterward?
