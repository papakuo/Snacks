    30. Whom the World Was Not Worthy (Hebrews 11: 32 - 40)

    The Starter

    Many individuals had shown their faith in the Bible. Even in the dark years of Judges. Because of faith, they didn't compromise with the world, the world suppressed them. So, they all suffered in various ways. How did they achieve faith to know that the world was not worthy of them? How do we receive the same faith to prevail in life?
