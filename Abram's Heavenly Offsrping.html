    10. Abram's Heavenly Offsrping (Genesis 15: 5 - 6)

    The Starter

    When someone makes promises to us, we want to see them get fulfilled, the sooner the better. When God made promises to Abram for the descendants and all the land, Abram would not be able to see them with his own eyes. Yet, that didn't bother him. He still lived in those promises and put his faith in God. That's why his faith could grow stronger day by day. How did we know that? Could we learn from that?
