    32. The Discipline (Hebrews 12: 3 - 10)

    The Starter

    After being saved, God also wants us to be sanctified and perfected so that we could be His holy and glorious legitimate sons. To do so, God disciplines us according to His will which would cause us the growing pain. Is there a way to mitigate the growing pain? Also, how do we know about God's disciplines to obey?
